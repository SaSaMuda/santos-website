The Maker & Problem Solver

Hello, I am Santos. While I am a trained Schreiner (carpenter) by trade, my true passion lies in being a versatile "manitas"—someone who solves practical problems with creativity and craftsmanship.

Working out of Tüftelwerk Luzern, I've expanded my toolkit far beyond wood to include metalworking, leathercraft, 3D printing, and electronics upcycling, amongst other things. Whether it's repairing a broken item, building a custom piece of furniture, or recycling e-bike batteries, my goal is always the same: highly functional solutions that also look great.

Through the process of these projects, I am learning new things while solving problems. With my background, I have the intangibles to achieve whatever I set my mind to.
